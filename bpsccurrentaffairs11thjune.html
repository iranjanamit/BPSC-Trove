Bihar Special: Leading Digital Health

This section highlights Bihar's significant achievements in public services, focusing on its top ranking in digital healthcare implementation and its leading position in maternal and newborn care. These advancements showcase the state's commitment to leveraging technology and robust systems to improve citizen well-being.

Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission

Bihar has secured the #1 national rank in the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission, setting new benchmarks for accessible and efficient public health delivery. This success is driven by a state-of-the-art Command and Control Centre in Patna, which monitors health facilities in real-time.

The system ensures prompt alerts, compliance monitoring, and proactive management of health emergencies by tracking disease trends and patient data.

Leader in Newborn & Maternal Care

According to the Poshan Tracker report from the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Directorate, Bihar has achieved the top position nationwide for the care of newborns and pregnant women, surpassing Delhi and Uttar Pradesh.

Bihar Rural League (BRL) 2024-25

The Bihar Cricket Association (BCA) has launched the Bihar Rural League (BRL) with the ambitious goal of discovering and nurturing cricketing talent from every village in the state, promoting sports at the grassroots level.

'Ayush Nivesh Saarthi' Portal

The Government of India has launched a digital, investor-centric platform to boost the traditional wellness sector. The portal aims to attract domestic and global investors by providing a single interface for policies, incentives, and projects.

Developed by the Ministry of Ayush and Invest India, it's designed to transform traditional wellness systems into a robust economic driver.

Defense & Important Days

Stay updated on key appointments within the Indian Army and recognize important international observances. This section features the new Deputy Chief of Army Staff and highlights the global celebration of the International Day of Play.

Lt General Rajiv Ghai Appointed

Lieutenant General Rajiv Ghai has been appointed as the new Deputy Chief of Army Staff (Strategy). He will also continue his role as Director General of Military Operations (DGMO).

A senior officer from the Kumaon Regiment, he was recently awarded the Uttam Yudh Seva Medal (UYSM) for his distinguished service, particularly in counter-insurgency operations in Jammu and Kashmir.
